About Us



We are Undergraduate Students from India motivated to contribute to the pandemic situation.

The portal that we are using now is currently having a lot of issues, and so we came up with an alternative Portal and App, to receive the best and the accurate info about the COVID-19 Vaccine Availability in India. This can be expanded further to a wide range of Medicine Availability after the present situation gets better.This is a portal that gets in Volunteers from every possible location where COVID-19 vaccine is available, and we get them to update it in this portal through their specialized App. To make it happen we need your support.

Support our initiative and help us take it forward and serve our country and also people in need.
